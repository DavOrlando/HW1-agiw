Enhanced Multimedia.

The elegant Dell 27 Monitor – S2715H makes your media come to life with enhanced visual and audio features for an immersive entertainment experience.

Enjoy vivid, cinematic-quality pictures across an ultra-wide 178°/178° viewing angle with Full HD resolution.
Let the powerful 2x9W integrated speakers provide you with a captivating, rich audio experience.
The ultra-narrow bezel improves dual-monitor and widescreen viewingi, allowing the thinnest possible edges to be placed side by side for a virtually borderless appearance.

Make it your own.

Adjust your monitor for a custom fit with flexible viewing angles and multiple connectivity options.

Stream audio directly from your smartphone or tablet, display content to your monitor and charge your devices at the same time with the convenient connectivity of an HDMI/MHL port.
Two additional USB 2.0 ports allow you the freedom of connecting a mouse, keyboard or other peripherals to your monitor for a customized experience.
View your content the way you want to with flexible viewing options that allow you to tilt your display 5° forward or 21° backward for a more comfortable angle.
Free up space on your desktop by detaching the panel and mounting your display on the wall with an optional mounting kit, or attach it to the available Dell Single Arm Monitor for a closer look. 

Connectivity Options

Ports and Slots:

Cables Included:
1. HDMI cable
2. USB cable

Reliable performance, eco-efficient features.

Environmentally-conscious design
The Dell 27 Monitor – S2715H meets the latest regulatory and environmental standards.

ENERGY STAR®
QualifiedEPEAT® Goldi Rated
TCO Certified Displays

Made with premium-quality materials, the S2715H is BFR/PVC-free (excluding external cables) and has an arsenic-free glass and mercury-free LED panel.

Enhanced power management
Manage power consumption wisely with PowerNap, enabled through the Dell Display Manager to either dim the monitor to the minimum brightness level or put it into sleep mode when it is not in use.

Get the most from your monitor.

Complete your Dell monitor experience. Your Dell 27 Monitor – S2715H is just the start. Look for these great accessories when finalizing your purchase.

Dell Single Monitor Arm – MSA14

Easily mount and adjust your VESA compatible Dell monitor for comfortable viewing while saving desk space.

Dell Wireless Keyboard and Mouse – KM714

Delivers high-quality, wireless performance in a modern design that allows for a clutter-free desktop experience.

Dell Premium Lock — LP500

Secure your monitor with a super-strong, carbon-tempered steel cable lock that easily attaches to your monitor’s standard security lock slot.

Expert Support

Relax knowing that your Dell monitor comes with next business day Advanced Exchangei if your warranty issue can’t be resolved over the phone. Get a higher level of support with Premium Support for monitors which includes 24x7i access to expert technicians for set-up, configuration, troubleshooting, and more, as well as next business day Advanced Exchange.
