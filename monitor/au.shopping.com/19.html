AOC e2450Swd 24" LCD Monitor

AOC introduces the 50-Series of attractive, slim monitors with clean styling and LED technology, priced to appeal to value-conscious consumers. LED backlights contain no toxic mercury (Hg) to contaminate the environment, and offer substantially lower power consumption than traditional monitors, further reduced with user-selectable power-saving display modes and switch-off timer. Although e2450Swd presents simple monitors with a focus on value, AOC has not skimped on display dynamics, with a vivid contrast ratio of 20,000,000:1 DCR ensuring clear visibility and vibrant images.
