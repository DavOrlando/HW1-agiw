Marketing Information

A value 19" surveillance display with rapid response time, quick signal-switching and robust video support for security and control centre environments.

For a cost-effective surveillance display solution, look no further than the AG Neovo SC-19P. A bright, high resolution full color screen with quick signal-switching and 3ms response time ensures sharp, smooth video playback. Looping BNC video inputs and NTSC/ PAL support maximise flexibility for system integration. And with built-in speakers, stylishly slim profile and VESA mountability, the SC-19P will look just as attractive as its amazingly affordable price.
